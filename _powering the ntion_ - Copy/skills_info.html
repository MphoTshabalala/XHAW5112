At Empowering the Nation, we recognize that skills are the foundation of personal growth, employability, and entrepreneurship. Our diverse range of training programmes is designed to equip domestic workers, gardeners, and other individuals with practical skills that elevate their professional capabilities. Whether you're seeking to enhance your expertise or expand your career opportunities, our courses are tailored to meet the unique demands of the industry while empowering individuals to reach their fullest potential. Our training programmes are broken into two main categories: *six-month Learnerships* and *six-week Short Skills Training Programmes*. Each course offers specialized instruction to help individuals master key skills that are not only in demand but also practical in everyday situations. Whether you're an employer looking to invest in your staff’s development or an individual aspiring to learn new skills, we offer a solution that is both accessible and affordable.

- *First Aid*: Providing critical knowledge in emergency scene management, CPR, and handling wounds, burns, and fractures. - *Sewing*: Offering a creative outlet while teaching key skills like alterations, new garment design, and using sewing machines to produce quality clothing. - *Landscaping*: Equipping participants with the knowledge to design and maintain beautiful outdoor spaces, balancing indigenous and exotic plants with structures like fountains and benches. - *Life Skills*: Teaching participants fundamental life skills such as literacy, numeracy, opening a bank account, and understanding basic labor laws to navigate everyday challenges. #### Six-Week Short Skills Training Programmes: For those looking for quick yet effective training, our six-week Short Skills Programmes provide the perfect solution. These focused courses cover specific skill sets that can be quickly learned and applied, ensuring that participants gain the practical knowledge needed to excel in their roles. Our short courses include: - *Child Minding*: Focused on providing care for children from birth to toddler age, teaching essential skills like baby needs, educational play, and safe practices. - *Cooking*: Teaching participants how to prepare healthy, nutritious family meals with an understanding of proper food groups and meal planning techniques. - *Garden Maintenance*: Covering essential gardening tasks such as pruning, watering, and planting, while educating on the specific needs of indigenous and exotic plants. Each course is structured to provide hands-on learning experiences, ensuring that every participant has the opportunity to practice and refine their skills. Our trainers are experienced professionals who provide personalized guidance to ensure that each trainee fully understands and can apply the skills learned. We also believe that investing in multiple skills is one of the best ways to enhance employability and income potential. For this reason, our pricing structure includes discounts for individuals enrolling in multiple courses. By combining courses, participants can not only broaden their skill sets but also enjoy significant savings. At Empowering the Nation, our goal is not just to train but to transform. Through our skill development programmes, we aim to unlock potential, inspire confidence, and create opportunities that change lives. Whether you're looking to upskill yourself or your team, our range of courses is designed to provide meaningful, marketable skills that make a real difference in the workforce. Invest in your future today by enrolling in one of our transformative courses, and join the hundreds of individuals who have already taken the next step towards personal and professional growth. Together, we are empowering a generation with the skills they need to succeed.
