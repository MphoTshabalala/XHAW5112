AIM:

Empowering the Nation aims to upskill domestic workers and gardeners by providing high-quality, practical training in various fields. The organization seeks to enable these workers to enhance their employability, command higher wages, and become entrepreneurs through newly acquired skills. Empowering the Nation also aims to support employers by offering skilled services within households.

Target:

The target audience includes domestic workers and gardeners who wish to improve their skill sets, as well as employers looking to upskill their household staff.
